Teeth Whitening

Tooth whitening is a great way to get back that stunning white smile and make you feel so much more confident around others.

Teeth age with time and they get less white as we get older. Thankfully tooth whitening is an easy, affordable way to get that fresh, young smile back.We provide a complimentary assessment & recommendation to all customers interested in our teeth whitening service.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics and Invisalign (a popular system) is a great way to get that great confident smile. If teeth are crowded a malpositioned, sometimes the only way to improve your smile is to delicately move the teeth into their best-desired positions.

Orthodontics can help in various ways . Sometimes a clear aligner system (like Invisalign) is the best system to use on a crowded teeth case, but sometimes a better result is achieved with a more conventional fixed “braces” system.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are well-accepted and long term solution for replacing missing teeth. Placed directly into the jaw bone, a dental implant provides a replacement for the root of a missing tooth or teeth. A dental implant can support crowns or dentures, in a similar way that roots support natural teeth.

Usually made from titanium, dental implants have an internal screw that holds false tooth (or teeth) in place. Once the implant is fitted, surrounding bone grows up and attaches to the implant, and keeps it in place.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is a broad term used to describe different dental techniques to restore your teeth and smile, with the focus being on achieving the best aesthetic, functional result.

Smile Makeover

Facial aesthetics are a safe and simple way to turn back the hands of time and reduce the impact of wrinkles and lines that come with age. These specialized, non-surgical procedures can tighten skin, smooth out creases, and reduce minor flaws. Facial aesthetic treatment can give you an overall more youthful appearance.

We offer a range of facial aesthetic treatments that can enhance your skin’s youthful appearance. We believe everyone deserves to look and feel their best, which is why we use only the highest quality products with proven safety records.
